On your actual question.

**Build Pack** ($15K–$40K, 4 weeks) is a fixed-scope project. You bring a client engagement, I build 3–5 production agents, install governance, hand you the keys. Day 30, agents are running.

**Fractional AI Operator** ($8K–$15K/mo, 6–12 months) is ongoing embedded delivery. I operate inside a client engagement 2–3 days a week, deploying agents monthly, reporting to the engagement lead.

The gap in pricing isn't about deliverables. It's about whether a client needs a system built once or a system operated ongoing. For a firm like IA Collaborative, the Build Pack slots into defined-scope engagements where the outcome is clear. The Fractional role fits longer client relationships where the AI layer needs continuous stewardship. Different clients, different levers.

From what you wrote, it sounds like you are evaluating which of these fits your delivery model — not which one to buy. That is the right question.
