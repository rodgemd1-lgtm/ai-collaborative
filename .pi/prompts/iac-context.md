# IA Collaborative — Master Context

## Identity & Why We Exist

**IA Collaborative's actual identity:** Insight to Action. A design + strategy + innovation consultancy founded 1991, headquartered Chicago, with offices in New York, San Francisco, and Bangalore. 200+ people. Co-CEOs: Dan Kraemer (Kellogg MBA) and Kathleen Brandenburg (Fast Co "Master of Design," Harvard visiting professor).

**IAC's current state (v0):**
- Revenue: ~$16M/year, 100% billable hours
- No software platform, no SaaS revenue, no platform multiple
- All work is custom SOW project delivery
- Fortune 500 clients only (Google, Apple, Nike, Airbnb, Allstate, etc.)

**Where they could be (v5+):**
- $50–100M ARR
- Recurring SaaS revenue (Forefront Platform)
- Productized services with SKU-level pricing
- Mid-market play ($50M–$500M companies)
- AI-native agentic infrastructure powering engagements
- 14× revenue multiple vs. current 1× professional services multiple

**Our thesis:** IAC has spent 25 years building world-class frameworks (IDEALS Model, Seven Elements, Collaborative Intelligence Stack). But those frameworks are IP delivered as consulting, not products. The 14× revenue gap between a custom SOW and a productized SKU is the opportunity.
