# Content Creation Workflow

**When:** Producing blog posts, insights, or social content.
**Trigger:** `pi-iac content`

## Content Pillars
1. **The Mid-Market Wedge** — $50–500M whitespace
2. **Framework as Product** — IDEALS, Seven Elements, Hexis → recurring revenue
3. **Speed as Strategy** — 8 weeks vs. 9 months
4. **Collaborative Intelligence** — Category IAC can own
5. **Proof Over Promise** — Live systems as evidence

## Gamma Deck Rules
- Max 12 slides
- Bullets + bold headlines only (no paragraphs on slides)
- Dark navy + white palette
- Big stat callouts: 31.6% CAGR, 12–18 months, $16M → $50M+
- Package pricing table on pricing slide
- Kathleen craft standard: clean, modern, strong white space

## Social Post Rules
**LinkedIn:** Framework anchor + 1-sentence insight + CTA
**X/Twitter:** Framework + 1 sentence + link + hashtags
