# AI Collaborative Agent Roster

| # | Agent | Role | Best For | Persona |
|---|-------|------|----------|---------|
| 1 | **Orchestrator** | Task router, verification, risk flags | Every session starts here | |

## Activation Map

| User says... | Primary Agent | Secondary | Why |
|--------------|---------------|-----------|-----|
| "Prepare for my Kyle meeting" | Client | Strategy + Research | Meeting prep runs recon, then structures the ask |
| "Run a Prism sim on X" | Research | Strategy | Simulation + synthesis |
| "Write a blog post about Y" | Content | Research | Content needs intel first |
| "Fix the website Z page" | Web | Content | Copy + layout both needed |
| "Check my Instantly campaign" | Campaigns | Client | Campaign health → pipeline impact |
| "What should I charge for X?" | Strategy | Client | Pricing needs client-context |
| "Research competitor Y" | Research | Strategy | Intel → positioning |

## Agent-to-Persona Mapping (for IAC audience)
When drafting client-facing material, choose the persona that matches the agent's output:
- Strategy outputs → STRATEGIST persona (Kathleen, Jeff love this)
- Web outputs → BUILDER persona (Rahul, technical reviewers)
- Client outputs → PARTNER persona (Kyle, Dan love this)
- Campaign outputs → CONSULTANT persona (traditional procurement)
- Research outputs → STRATEGIST persona (executive briefings)
- Content outputs → STRATEGIST persona (thought leadership)
